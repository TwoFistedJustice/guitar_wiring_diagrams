The lone toggle switch is phase reverse.
Upper contacts: White is hot, black is ground
Lower contacts: Black is hot, white is ground

Vol PP:
Up: Bridge ON, bypass tone pot go straight to volume
Down: Bridge subject to 5-way switch, tone pot active on bridge

Tone PP:
Both positions: Greasebucket
Up: 22nf cap
Down: 47nf or 68nf cap
